Greetings, Esteemed Apprentices of the Mystical Arts

Within this enchanted grimoire, you will find spells of the highest order.

Chapter 1: Elemental Spells

Fireball

A fiery projectile that engulfs the target in flames.

Ice Shard

A sharp icicle that freezes enemies on impact.

Chapter 2: Summoning Spells

Summon Familiar

Conjures a loyal creature to aid the caster.

Summon Elemental

Brings forth an elemental spirit to serve the caster.
